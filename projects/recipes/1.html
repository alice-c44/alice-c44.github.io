Stir Fried Rice (蛋炒饭)

This Recipe

Stir fried rice or 蛋炒饭(dan chao fan) is a staple dish in most chinese households. It's very simple and easy to make, and perfect when you have leftover rice or ingredients. This specific recipe is to make egg stir fried rice but there are so many different combinations you can make to create a whole new dish. I'll list of couple of my favorites down below.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup frozen corn
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp umami (mushroom) powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 cups cooked rice

Directions

  1. Heat a pan on high heat and add around 1/2 tbsp of cooking oil.
  2. Add corn and peas and stirfry until no longer frozen. Set aside in a plate.
  3. Beat the eggs and scramble on medium heat.
  4. Add the rice, corn and peas.
  5. Add soy sauce, umami powder and onion powder and mix until everything is seasoned.
Variations
Meat 1 cup of any cooked ground meat. Cook with soy sauce and 1 tsp 5-Spice powder.
Spice 1 cup chopped kimchi, add with rice.
